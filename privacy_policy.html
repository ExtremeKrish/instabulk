Privacy Policy for Instabulk

1. Information Collection

We collect the following information from users:

  • Image file uploaded by the user.
  • Social media handle name.
  • Username.

2. Collection Method

Information is collected through user inputs and stored locally in the user's browser using cookies and local storage.

3. Purpose of Collection

We collect this information to facilitate a personalized experience. When users reload the page, the imported information populates the respective fields.

4. Data Storage

No information is stored on our servers. All data is saved locally on the user's device through cookies and local storage.

5. Information Usage

The collected information is solely used to enhance user experience and is not shared with any third parties.

6. Data Security

We prioritize user data security. Information stored locally is protected through standard security measures.

7. User Choices

Users have the option to clear cookies or disable local storage in their browser settings if they choose not to store this information.

8. Cookies and Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for the purpose of improving user interactions and experience on the website.

9. Contact Information

Users can contact us through the contact page, email, Telegram channel, and Discord server for any privacy concerns or queries.

This privacy policy is effective as of 14 Jan 2024 and may be updated as needed.
